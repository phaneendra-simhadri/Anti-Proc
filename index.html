One For All — How AntiProc Works

Deku didn't become the greatest hero by half-trying. Neither will you. Read the rules once. Follow them always.

01
3 Non-Negotiable Targets

Like Deku's training regimes — 3 new tasks for today. All done before midnight. Mark incomplete tasks ↩ Carry to bring them to tomorrow.

02
Finalize & Lock Results

Click “Finalize Day” to lock in. All 3 done → streak extends, +30 XP. Any missed → streak resets, −20 XP, penalty +15.

03
Deep Work Blocks (60–90 min)

One For All requires full commitment. Log your training sessions: planned vs actual minutes. Efficiency = (actual ÷ planned) − interruption cost.

04
Streak, XP & Hero Rank

Consecutive perfect days grow your streak (OFA glow at 3+). XP unlocks ranks: Quirkless → U.A. Hopeful → Hero Course → ... → Symbol of Peace.

05
Weekly Debrief (every 7 days)

After every 7 finalized days, a debrief unlocks — like Deku's notebooks. Write honestly: what you executed, where you failed, what changes next week.

💾
Data & Backup

All data lives in your browser's localStorage. Export saves a JSON backup. Import restores it. Reset requires typing “RESET” to prevent accidents.
